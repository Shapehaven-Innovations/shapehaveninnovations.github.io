Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 06, 2025

ShapeHaven Innovations LLC (“ShapeHaven,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) operates one or more mobile applications (each an “App”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any data handled in connection with your use of our App(s). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information, including details required by Apple and additional language for third-party advertising.

1. Scope & Applicability

This Privacy Policy applies to data collected or processed directly by ShapeHaven Innovations LLC in connection with your use of any of our App(s). By installing or using our App(s), you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. No Personal Data Collection by ShapeHaven

  1. User-Generated Content & Preferences:
    • Any presets, configuration settings, or histories you create within the App are stored locally on your device (e.g., using Apple’s UserDefaults or local file storage).
    • We do not transmit that content or those settings to any external server.
  2. Device & Usage Data (Technical Only):
    • To keep the App functional and up-to-date, Apple’s iOS system may record basic diagnostic data or crash logs (if you have enabled diagnostics & usage sharing). ShapeHaven does not access or view those logs—Apple’s diagnostics infrastructure alone handles them.
    • We do not embed any third-party analytics, metrics, or monitoring frameworks (e.g., Firebase, Google Analytics).

3. In-App Purchases & Payment Processing

Our App(s) may offer an auto-renewable subscription or other in-app purchases for premium features or content. All billing and payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple through the App Store. We never see, store, or manage your credit card or billing information.

Apple’s Transaction Data:

  • When you make a purchase or renew a subscription, Apple’s systems collect information necessary to complete the transaction (e.g., your Apple ID, purchase receipt, billing country).
  • ShapeHaven only receives confirmation from Apple that a valid purchase or subscription exists; we do not receive any of your payment details.
  • For details on how Apple handles your purchase data—what they collect, how they retain it, and how long they keep it—refer to Apple’s Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/resources/

4. Data Retention & Server Information

  1. ShapeHaven’s Servers:
    • Data Location: We do not operate any backend servers for our App(s), and no user-identifiable data is ever transmitted off your device.
    • Data Retention: Since all user-generated data remains on your device, ShapeHaven has no server-side retention obligations.
  2. Apple’s Servers (for Purchases):
    • Apple retains purchase receipts and basic purchase metadata on secure servers.
    • You can review or request deletion of that purchase data via your Apple ID account settings.
    • For details on Apple’s data retention policies, see Apple’s Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/resources/

5. Third-Party Services & Links

App Store (Apple) Services:

  • The App Store handles our App(s) distribution, updates, purchase validation, and (if you’ve opted in) crash-reporting diagnostics. Any analytics or crash logs generated by Apple’s operating system or by TestFlight (in the case of beta testing) are governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy. ShapeHaven does not access those logs directly.

External URLs:

  • If our App includes links to external websites (e.g., for support, documentation, or Terms of Use), those sites may collect information according to their own policies. Always review the privacy statements of any third-party website before providing personal data.

5.1 Third-Party Advertising & Analytics (AdMob)

If we integrate Google AdMob (or any other ad network) in the future, that SDK may collect certain device and usage data for personalized ads and analytics. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Device Identifiers: e.g. IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers), device advertising settings.
  • App Usage Events: e.g. which screens you view, how long you stay, ad impressions and clicks.
  • Location Data: if you grant location permission at the system level.

Google/AdMob uses this information to serve targeted advertisements, measure ad performance, and improve its services. For details about how Google collects, uses, and shares your information when you use apps with Google services, see: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized ads by:

  1. Going to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking on your iPhone or iPad and toggling off “Allow Apps to Request to Track,” or
  2. Adjusting your device’s Limit Ad Tracking setting under Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising, or
  3. Visiting Google’s Ads Settings page: https://adssettings.google.com/

Disabling “Allow Apps to Request to Track” or “Limit Ad Tracking” will not prevent you from seeing ads, but they will be less relevant.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our App(s) are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not collect any personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has inadvertently provided any personal information through our App(s), please delete the App from the device.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  1. Local Data Management:
    Because user-generated data (presets, histories, etc.) is stored on your device, you may delete the App or clear its data at any time via Settings → General → iPhone Storage → App Name → Delete App. That action erases all local data for that App.
  2. Opting Out of Diagnostics Sharing:
    To prevent Apple from sending diagnostic & usage data related to our App(s) to Apple, go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements and toggle off “Share iPhone Analytics” or “Share App Analytics.”
  3. In-App Purchases Management:
    You can manage, cancel, or modify any subscription at any time via Settings → Subscriptions → App Name. Apple’s system will handle the rest.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time (for example, if Apple introduces new requirements or if we add features that collect data). When we do, we will:

  • Update the “Last updated” date at the top.
  • Post the revised Privacy Policy within our App(s) (e.g., in a “Settings → Privacy Policy” screen) and on our website (if one exists).

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our App(s) after changes are posted indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
